## The WaveMotion Mechanism

At the LOKI Wave Pro's core is WaveMotion — the same technology used in the SORAYA Wave for G-spot stimulation. The prostate arm's tip performs a rhythmic come-hither rocking motion, pressing against the prostate repeatedly in a way that no vibration pattern can replicate.

The physical sensation of mechanical movement against the prostate is categorically different from vibration. Many experienced users describe prostate orgasms via WaveMotion as more intense and distinctly different from vibration-induced ones.

WaveMotion has three speeds. At its slowest, it's meditative — a slow rhythmic rocking. At maximum, it's vigorous and immediately noticeable.

Running all four motors simultaneously — WaveMotion + internal vibration + both perineum motors — creates a sensation of extraordinary density. ## Ergonomics and Fit

The LOKI Wave Pro is a large, well-engineered piece of silicone hardware. It seats securely during use — the external arm maintains perineum contact even during movement. The curve is calibrated for typical prostate position.

Controls: two panels, one for the internal mechanisms and one for the perineum motors. Each panel has two buttons. Clear labeling, but there is a learning curve to operating four motors simultaneously during use. A remote control (like the HUGO has) would improve usability — this is the LOKI Wave Pro's main design weakness.

## Noise

The WaveMotion mechanism produces soft mechanical sound at higher speeds — similar to the SORAYA Wave. The vibration motors add some hum. Not silent, but not intrusive in a private setting.

## Battery Life

Full charge in 2 hours, approximately 90 minutes of use across all motors. For extended sessions using all four motors at high intensity, 90 minutes is adequate but not generous.

## LOKI Wave Pro vs HUGO

Both are LELO's premium prostate toys. The HUGO has dual motors (prostate + perineum) and a remote control. The LOKI Wave Pro has four motors including WaveMotion but no remote. The remote control makes the HUGO significantly better for partner play. The WaveMotion makes the LOKI Wave Pro significantly better for solo sessions where intensity is the priority. At $249 vs $219, the LOKI Wave Pro costs $30 more for substantially more stimulation complexity.
