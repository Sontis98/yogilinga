The Lovense Calor is the first app-controlled male masturbator with active heating built in — and it turns out that heating matters more than most toys in this category acknowledge.

At body temperature (up to 40°C / 104°F), the silicone sleeve of the Calor produces a warmth that meaningfully changes the tactile experience. Combined with Lovense's established vibration technology and full app control, the Calor makes a case that temperature is an underexplored dimension in sex toy design.

## What Makes the Calor Different

Most male masturbators — including the Max 2 — don't address the temperature gap between a warm body and a room-temperature toy. The Calor addresses it directly: a built-in heating element warms the interior sleeve to approximately body temperature before and during use.

This is not a gimmick. The sensory difference is immediate and significant on first use.

The Calor also vibrates (5 intensity levels, 10 vibration patterns) and integrates fully with the Lovense Remote app, allowing partner control over distance — the same app ecosystem used by Lush 3, Nora, and Max 2.

## How It Works

**Heating system:** Press the heat button before use. The Calor warms to approximately 40°C in about 5 minutes and maintains that temperature during use. The sleeve exterior stays warm; the interior reaches the target temperature quickly.

**Vibration motor:** Positioned toward the base of the device, the vibration transmits through the full sleeve. The Calor doesn't have the Max 2's air-contraction mechanism — it relies on vibration plus the heating element. For users who prioritize the contraction feature, the Max 2 remains the better choice; for users who prioritize thermal realism, the Calor wins decisively.

**Sleeve design:** The insertable sleeve is textured with a realistic internal structure and made from body-safe silicone. It's removable for cleaning. The opening is generous enough for most users.

## App & Connectivity

Full Lovense Remote integration. A partner can control vibration intensity and patterns from any location. Sound-activation mode, custom pattern creation, and interactive content platform access all work the same as other Lovense toys.

Battery life is approximately 60 minutes of combined heat + vibration at moderate intensity. Heat alone uses less battery; vibration adds drain. Charge time is around 70 minutes via USB.

## Build & Materials

**Body:** Hard ABS plastic exterior with a silicone sleeve. The device is well-balanced and easy to hold during use. Weight is light enough to use one-handed comfortably.

**Sleeve:** Removable, body-safe silicone. Non-porous, easy to clean. The Calor fits users up to average girth comfortably; significantly above-average girth may find the fit tight.

**Heating element:** Embedded in the device body, not the sleeve itself — so heat transfers through the plastic and silicone into the interior. This design keeps the heating element dry and protected.

## Cleaning

Remove the sleeve after each use. Wash with warm water and unscented soap, dry completely. Don't use silicone-based lubricant — use water-based only. The ABS exterior wipes clean.

## Calor vs Max 2

The Max 2 and Calor are the two flagship Lovense masturbators at the same price. The Max 2 has air-contraction; the Calor has heating. They fill different niches rather than competing directly.

If the realistic warmth of body temperature matters to you, get the Calor. If you want the unique contraction + sync-with-partner feature, get the Max 2. If you can only get one, the choice depends entirely on which feature resonates more — both are exceptional for $99.

## Our Verdict

The Lovense Calor solves a problem that most masturbators don't try to solve: thermal realism. The heating system works well, warms quickly, and makes a genuine difference. The Lovense app is the best in the category, and the full ecosystem integration with partner toys makes the Calor more than a solo device.

The only things it doesn't have compared to the Max 2 are the contraction mechanism and sync-with-partner response mode. Everything else is at least equal.
