The Lovense Ridge occupies a unique position in the lineup: it's designed to fit over a penis or a strap-on dildo, making it the most genuinely versatile toy in Lovense's catalog. For queer couples, non-binary users, or anyone who uses prosthetic toys, the Ridge is the first Lovense product specifically designed to include them.

For penis owners, it functions as a vibrating sleeve with a ribbed exterior texture that transmits sensation both ways — to the wearer and to a partner receiving penetration.

## Design

The Ridge is a stretchy silicone sleeve with pronounced external ribbing. The ribs create texture for receiving partners during use, and the vibration motor is embedded in the sleeve itself, so both the internal wearer/user and an external partner feel the vibrations.

**Versatility:**
- Over a penis: vibration felt internally by the wearer and by a receiving partner
- Over a strap-on: vibration transmitted to both partners — the toy wearer (through the harness or directly) and the receiving partner

The sleeve stretches to accommodate most sizes. The silicone is softer than most hard-shelled toys — comfortable for both giving and receiving partners.

## Vibration

The motor is positioned mid-sleeve to distribute vibration across the full length. This means receiving partners feel vibration along the contact surface rather than just at one end — more diffuse and more comprehensive than a tip-mounted motor.

Vibration intensity is strong enough to be felt clearly by both partners simultaneously. Five intensity levels plus custom patterns via app.

## App Control

Full Lovense Remote integration. Either partner controls intensity and patterns from their phone. For couples where both partners are present, this creates an interactive dynamic — the receiving partner can control the intensity of their own stimulation, or hand control to the giving partner.

Long-distance use: for couples who use strap-on toys long-distance via video, the Ridge allows remote control of the toy's vibration from any location.

## Build & Care

Body-safe silicone throughout. The sleeve is easy to clean — remove, wash with warm water and mild soap, dry completely. Compatible with water-based lubricant only (silicone lube damages silicone sleeves).

IPX7 waterproof. The charging connector is magnetic USB.

## Who Is the Ridge Best For?

- Couples who use strap-ons and want mutual vibration stimulation
- Penis owners who want sleeve-style vibration with texture their partner also feels
- Anyone in the Lovense ecosystem looking for a more inclusive unisex toy
- Queer and non-binary users specifically underserved by most vibrator brands

The Lovense Ridge fills a genuine gap in the market — an app-controlled vibrating sleeve that works as well on a strap-on as on a penis. The execution is solid: good vibration strength, quality silicone, and the full Lovense app ecosystem. For the users it was designed for, it's the best option available.
