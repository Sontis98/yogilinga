## What Is the b-Vibe Rimming Plug 2?

The b-Vibe Rimming Plug 2 is one of the most mechanically distinctive anal plugs available. Its neck contains **three small stainless steel beads that rotate** — creating a sensation against the sphincter that mimics external rimming. The body also contains a vibrating motor for internal stimulation.

The two mechanisms operate independently: rotation speed and direction (both directions supported) plus vibration intensity and pattern are all adjustable. The combination creates layered anal stimulation unlike anything a simple vibrating plug produces.

## The Rotating Bead Mechanism

This is the Rimming Plug 2's core feature. Three steel beads embedded in the silicone neck rotate in a circular pattern, pressing gently against the internal sphincter as they move. The sensation is unmistakable — a rolling, orbiting pressure at the entry point.

Rotation speeds: three levels. At low, it's a gentle, almost meditative sensation. At maximum, the rotation is noticeable and active. Direction reversal adds variety — clockwise and counterclockwise feel subtly different.

The beads are fully encased in silicone — there are no exposed metal parts, no gaps for bacteria to enter. The mechanism is sealed and waterproof.

## Vibration Component

A separate motor in the plug body provides traditional vibration — five intensity levels, three patterns. The vibration is good quality for this price point — rumbly rather than buzzy, transmitting into the internal tissue effectively.

Running both rotation and vibration simultaneously produces a complex, layered anal stimulation. Most users find that alternating between the two mechanisms (rotation to build arousal, vibration to intensify) works better than maxing both at once.

## Size and Materials

Insertable length: 9cm. Maximum diameter: 3.8cm. This is an intermediate-to-advanced size — not recommended as a first plug. The Rimming Plug Petite is available for smaller entry-level sizing.

100% platinum-cured silicone body, stainless steel internal beads. USB rechargeable — approximately 1.5 hours of use per charge, 2-hour charge time. Waterproof (IPX5).

## Noise

The rotating mechanism produces an audible low rumble — more noticeable than a simple vibrator. Not loud enough to be heard through walls in normal circumstances, but noticeable to the user. If near-silence is a priority, look elsewhere.

## Remote Control

The Rimming Plug 2 comes with a wireless remote — a significant usability addition for self-use and essential for partner play. The remote handles both mechanisms. Range is approximately 10 metres.
