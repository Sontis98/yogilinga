The Dame Kip is a bullet vibrator that takes discretion seriously. Its cylindrical shape, available in muted colors, reads as a cosmetics item or marker — not a sex toy. For users in shared living situations or who travel frequently, this matters practically.

The motor is Dame's standard: rumbly rather than buzzy, with genuine depth of vibration. The Kip isn't just a pretty bullet — it delivers.

---

## Form Factor

The Kip is 4 inches long and roughly the diameter of a thick marker. The smooth, consistent cylinder shape is intentionally neutral — no phallic connotations, no obviously erotic silhouette.

**Available colors:** Muted, desaturated tones (dusty rose, sage, slate) that blend with cosmetics or stationery rather than standing out.

The flat tip allows for precise, targeted stimulation on a specific point. Some users prefer this pinpoint precision; others prefer the broader stimulation of a wand-style toy. The Kip is for users who want targeted contact.

---

## Motor

Dame's rumbly motor in compact form. The Kip's vibration is deeper and more tissue-penetrating than cheap bullet vibrators at this size. Comparable to the We-Vibe Tango X in vibration character, though slightly less powerful at maximum.

**3 speeds, 4 patterns.** Simple enough to operate one-handed.

---

## Waterproofing

IPX7 — fully submersible. The Kip is designed for bath and shower use, and the construction supports this reliably. The single-piece body with no seams or gaps is the reason this level of waterproofing is achievable.

---

## Charging

USB-C magnetic charging. Battery life is approximately 60–90 minutes at moderate intensity — sufficient for typical use. Charges fully in about 90 minutes.

---

## Travel

The Kip's shape makes it the easiest sex toy to travel with. It fits in a toiletry bag and doesn't look out of place. The magnetic cap protects the charging point. TSA-friendly (it genuinely looks like a cosmetic item).

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Users who value discretion, travel frequently, or want a bullet vibrator for shower use. Also a good choice for users who prefer targeted, precise stimulation over broad contact.

**Choose We-Vibe Tango X if:** Maximum motor power is the priority — the Tango X edges the Kip in raw intensity.

## Verdict

The best discreet bullet vibrator. Dame delivers their characteristic motor quality in a form that genuinely blends into daily life. Worth $75 for users who value the discretion and waterproofing.
