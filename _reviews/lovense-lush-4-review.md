The Lovense Lush series is the best-selling line of wearable vibrators ever made. The Lush 3 sat at the top of every reputable ranking for three years. The Lush 4 is the iteration that addresses the two complaints the Lush 3 actually had: motor power and wearability during active use.

Everything else was already excellent. The Lush 4 doesn't reinvent the formula — it refines it to the point where the competition has genuinely fallen behind.

## What Changed From Lush 3 to Lush 4

**Motor:** The Lush 4 has a noticeably stronger motor than the Lush 3. The lowest setting is roughly equivalent to the Lush 3's mid-level. This matters for users who found the Lush 3's top settings insufficient — the Lush 4's top settings are in a different league.

**Retention:** The Lush 4's body geometry is slightly adjusted to improve retention during walking, dancing, or active partnered sex. It's not a radical change but it's a meaningful one. The Lush 3 could shift out of position during vigorous movement; the Lush 4 stays put more reliably.

**Charging:** Updated magnetic charging connector — faster charge time (around 60 minutes for full charge).

**Noise:** Marginally quieter than the Lush 3, though both are already among the quietest in their category.

## Core Features

The Lush 4 remains a Bluetooth-connected wearable egg vibrator controlled entirely through the Lovense Remote app. The core use case is unchanged: insert the egg, wear it under clothing, and let a partner control it from their phone — whether in the same room or on the other side of the world.

**App ecosystem:** Full Lovense Remote integration. Partner control, music sync (toy vibrates to audio), interactive content, custom pattern creation. The Lovense app remains the gold standard in this category — intuitive, reliable, feature-rich.

**Long-distance control:** An internet connection allows a partner to control the Lush 4 from any location with essentially zero latency. Video integration lets them see your reaction in real time.

**Sync:** Can be synced with other Lovense toys — notably the Max 2 and Gush 2 for long-distance couples where each partner has a toy.

## Wearability in Practice

The Lush 4 is designed to be worn during daily activities: walking, working, dining out. The egg sits internally; the tail sits against the external anatomy to hold position and transmit the control signal.

In our testing: the Lush 4 can be worn comfortably for extended periods. The motor is quiet enough for most public settings at lower intensities. For most users, the Lush 4 is genuinely undetectable at moderate settings.

The tail occasionally catches on underwear fabric — this is inherent to the design and present in all Lush versions.

## Build & Materials

Body-safe silicone throughout. IPX7 waterproof. The egg itself is smooth silicone; the antenna tail is flexible silicone. Use water-based lubricant only.

## Lush 4 vs Lush 3

If you already own a Lush 3 and it satisfies you, the upgrade is optional. If you want stronger vibration or have ever wished the Lush 3 stayed in place better, the Lush 4 is the upgrade. If you've never owned a Lush, there's no reason to consider the Lush 3 — the Lush 4 is the current version.

## Lush 4 vs Competitors

The We-Vibe Moxie+ ($139) is the main alternative. The Moxie+ uses magnetic attachment rather than insertion for its clitoral stimulator, which some users prefer. The Lush 4's motor is stronger; the Moxie+'s positioning against the clitoris may be more reliable for some body types. Both are excellent — the choice depends on whether insertable or external stimulation works better for you.
