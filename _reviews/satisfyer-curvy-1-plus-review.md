The Satisfyer Curvy 1+ is a step up from the entry-level Pro 2 and Pro Penguin in two significant ways: it adds a vibration motor alongside the air pulse mechanism, and it adds app connectivity for partner control.

The result is a toy that offers two distinct sensation types — air pulse and vibration — independently controllable and combinable.

---

## Dual Stimulation: Air Pulse + Vibration

The Curvy 1+ has two separate systems:

**Air pulse:** 11 intensity settings of the same pressure-wave stimulation found in the Pro 2. Same sensation profile, same effectiveness.

**Vibration motor:** Located in the toy's body, providing additional stimulation. The vibration motor is decent — rumbly enough to be pleasant, though not at We-Vibe's level of quality.

**Combined mode:** Running both simultaneously produces a layered sensation that many users find more effective than either alone. The combination can be overwhelming at high intensities — start with both at medium before increasing.

---

## Satisfyer App

The Curvy 1+ connects to the Satisfyer app via Bluetooth. The app is functional — reliable connection, remote control of both systems — but less polished than We-Vibe's We-Connect. The interface requires more navigation to change settings during use.

**Long-distance control:** Available via internet relay. Adequate for long-distance couples; latency is slightly higher (~300ms) than We-Vibe's implementation.

---

## Ergonomics

The curved shape fits naturally in the hand. The toy's body rests against the palm, with the nozzle positioned toward the clitoris naturally. Controls are accessible with the thumb during use.

---

## Build Quality

A noticeable upgrade from the entry-level Satisfyers. The silicone is thicker and softer, the plastic housing is more solid, and the overall finish is more refined. Still below We-Vibe and Womanizer's build standard, but meaningfully better than the Pro 2.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Users who've tried air pulse and want more, users who want both air pulse and vibration in one toy, or couples who want app-based remote control without spending We-Vibe prices.

**Upgrade to We-Vibe Chorus if:** Couples play and build quality are the primary criteria.

---

## Verdict

The best mid-range Satisfyer. The dual stimulation combination is genuinely effective, the app works, and the build quality justifies the step up from entry-level. Strong value at $55.

**Score: 8.5/10**
