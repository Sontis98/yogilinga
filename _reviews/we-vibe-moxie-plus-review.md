## How It Works

The Moxie+ clips magnetically to the inside of underwear, positioning the vibrating surface against the clitoris. The magnetic clip holds reliably during normal movement — walking, sitting, dancing — without the toy shifting or falling.

Partner control is via the We-Connect app: your partner can change intensity and patterns in real time from anywhere in the world with an internet connection.

## Motor Quality

Consistent with We-Vibe's standard: rumbly, deep vibration rather than surface buzz. The Moxie+ is quieter than many panty vibrators — the motor is damped well by the toy's housing. At low-to-medium intensities, the sound is masked by ambient restaurant or café noise.

At high intensities, the toy is audible in quiet environments. For truly silent public play, keep it at medium.

## App & Partner Control

We-Connect app — the same as the Chorus. Clean UI, reliable Bluetooth, and internet relay for long-distance control. Latency is ~100ms locally, ~250ms over internet. Responsive enough for real-time partner play.

**Sound activation mode:** The toy's vibration responds to ambient sound — conversation, music, crowd noise. An unusual feature that works better than expected in practice.

## Fit Across Underwear Types

The magnetic clip works with most underwear styles. It works best with mid-rise briefs and boyshorts — the clip needs enough fabric to hold. Thongs and very thin underwear provide less stability.

**One limitation:** The Moxie+ is an external toy only. It doesn't insert. If you want wearable internal stimulation, the We-Vibe Chorus or Lovense Lush 4 are better options.
