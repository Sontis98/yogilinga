The Satisfyer Love Triangle occupies an interesting market position: a wearable couples toy that uses air pulse stimulation rather than traditional vibration. It's the air pulse equivalent of the We-Vibe Chorus — designed for wear during penetrative sex, with the stimulating surface positioned against the clitoris.

It doesn't match the Chorus's build quality or app polish, but at $65 vs. $199, the comparison is unfair. For couples interested in air pulse stimulation during sex, this is the most accessible entry point.

---

## How It Works

The Love Triangle's triangular silicone body sits between partners during penetrative sex. The air pulse nozzle is positioned against the clitoris, delivering pressure-wave stimulation during intercourse.

Unlike the Chorus's C-shape (which has an internal component), the Love Triangle is entirely external — it sits in the space between partners, held in position by body contact and the natural geometry of the position.

---

## Air Pulse During Intercourse

The sensation of air pulse stimulation during penetrative sex is distinct from vibration. The pressure waves are less likely to transfer to a partner (a common complaint with vibrating couples toys), making the stimulation more targeted.

**Fit note:** The Love Triangle works best in missionary-adjacent positions where the partners' bodies maintain contact. In positions with more space between partners, the toy may shift. Experiment with positioning before use.

---

## Satisfyer App

Same app as the Curvy 1+. Reliable Bluetooth connection, adequate partner control, long-distance functionality via internet relay. Less polished than We-Connect but functional.

**Music sync mode:** The air pulse rhythm responds to music from your phone's microphone — a novelty feature that works better than expected and is genuinely fun for the right occasion.

---

## Build Quality

Solid for the price point. The silicone is soft and skin-safe; the housing is sturdy. The charging port is magnetically connected and easy to align. IPX6 waterproof.

The overall finish is a step below the We-Vibe Chorus but above entry-level Satisfyer products.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Couples who want to try air pulse stimulation during intercourse, or couples who've found traditional vibrating couples toys too transferable to the penetrating partner.

**Consider the We-Vibe Chorus if:** Build quality and app refinement matter more than price, or if you want internal G-spot stimulation alongside clitoral stimulation.

---

## Verdict

A genuinely innovative couples toy at an accessible price. Air pulse stimulation during intercourse is a distinct and effective sensation that the Love Triangle delivers reliably. Strong value for the category.
