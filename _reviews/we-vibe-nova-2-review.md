The We-Vibe Nova 2 addresses this with a **flexible clitoral arm** that moves independently of the internal shaft. During thrusting, the arm maintains contact with the clitoris rather than shifting away. It works — not perfectly for every anatomy, but better than any other rabbit we've tested.

## The Flexible Arm: How It Works

The Nova 2's external arm is attached to the body with a flexible hinge rather than a rigid connection. As the internal shaft moves during thrusting, the external arm flexes to maintain its position against the clitoris.

The result: dual stimulation that persists through movement rather than requiring you to stay completely still. This is the fundamental innovation that separates the Nova 2 from most rabbit vibrators.

**Anatomy caveat:** The arm works best when the distance between your vaginal opening and clitoris falls in the mid-range. Shorter or longer distances may require more adjustment. The Nova 2 fits more anatomies than most rabbits, but no rabbit vibrator is universal.
