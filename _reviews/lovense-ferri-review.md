The category of "panty vibrators" has a design problem. Most rely on fabric pockets or harnesses to hold the toy in place — systems that work in theory and slip in practice.

The Lovense Ferri solves this differently. It uses a **magnetic clip** that sandwiches the gusset of underwear between two magnets, holding the vibrator against the clitoris without straps, pockets, or harnesses. After four weeks of testing including actual outdoor use, the mechanism works better than we expected.

## Design & Wearability

The Ferri is shaped like a small teardrop — flat on the underwear-facing side, curved on the body-facing side, with a tapered tip that positions against the clitoris. The magnetic attachment clips onto the crotch panel of underwear from both sides.
