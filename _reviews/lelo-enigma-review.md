## What Is the LELO ENIGMA?

Most dual-stimulation toys vibrate on both arms. The ENIGMA produces sonic pulse waves on both arms — waves that travel into tissue rather than buzzing on the surface.

For the clitoral arm, this replicates the sensation of the SONA: a broad, deep stimulation of the full clitoral structure (including the internal branches). Starting with the external arm only replicates the SONA experience: deep, contactless sonic stimulation with no surface buzz. Adding the internal arm creates the ENIGMA's signature sensation — two sources of wave stimulation converging simultaneously on interconnected structures.
