The Gush 2 is a different type of toy from the Max 2 or Solace Pro. Where those are enclosed masturbators, the Gush 2 is a vibrating sleeve designed to wrap around the penis and stimulate the frenulum and shaft directly.

Think of it as a vibrator for penis owners rather than a masturbator — it's held against the body, not inserted into a sleeve. The distinction matters for users who find enclosed masturbators claustrophobic, who want something lighter and less commitment-heavy, or who want a toy that pairs easily with partnered activity.

## Design

The Gush 2 is a soft silicone sleeve with an opening at one end. It wraps around the penis rather than fully enclosing it — the frenulum (the sensitive underside of the glans) contacts the vibration motor directly. This design targets frenulum stimulation specifically, which many users find more reliably effective than diffuse vibration along a full sleeve.

The sleeve is flexible and adjustable. It accommodates a wide range of sizes without the fit concerns that apply to fixed-diameter masturbators.

## Vibration

The Gush 2's motor is positioned to deliver maximum vibration to the frenulum. The sensation is direct and focused rather than diffuse — more similar to how a vibrator is used on the clitoris than how a masturbator stimulates internally.

At higher intensities, the vibration is strong and consistently stimulating. At lower intensities, it's suitable for extended sessions or building intensity gradually.

## App Control

Full Lovense Remote integration. Partner control over vibration intensity and patterns. Sound-activation mode. Long-distance control over internet.

For couples: the Gush 2 + Lush 4 combination enables long-distance mutual stimulation — each partner has a Lovense toy, each partner controls the other's. This is the Lovense ecosystem's core couple use case, and the Gush 2 is the most accessible entry point for penis owners.

## Hands-Free Use

Partially hands-free is achievable — the sleeve can be held in place with light pressure rather than active gripping. Fully hands-free requires creative positioning (lying down with the sleeve between the body and a surface) but is possible.

For fully hands-free use with more active stimulation, the Max 2 or Solace Pro are more suited.

## Gush 2 vs Max 2

Different toys for different preferences. The Max 2 encloses fully and uses contraction + vibration; the Gush 2 wraps and focuses on frenulum vibration. Users who prefer targeted frenulum stimulation over full sleeve sensation will prefer the Gush 2. The Gush 2 is also easier to clean and less intimidating for first-time toy users.

At $89 vs $99, the price difference is minimal — the choice should be made on stimulation preference.

## Gush 2 vs Original Gush

The Gush 2 has a stronger motor and updated silicone formula compared to the original. If you've used the original Gush, the upgrade is meaningful. If you haven't tried either, the Gush 2 is the current version.

## Who Is the Gush 2 Best For?

- First-time buyers of vibrators for penis owners — lower complexity, easier to use
- Users who prefer targeted frenulum stimulation over full-sleeve sensation
- Couples who want app-connected mutual stimulation (paired with Lush 4 or Ferri)
- Anyone who finds enclosed masturbators uncomfortable or over-engineered

## Our Verdict

The Lovense Gush 2 fills a distinct gap in the lineup — it's the vibrator-style experience for penis owners that the Max 2 and Solace Pro's masturbator format doesn't serve. Frenulum-focused vibration, full app control, clean silicone build, and accessible price make it the most approachable Lovense toy for penis owners.

**Recommended for:** First-time buyers, frenulum stimulation preference, couples wanting paired app toys.
**Skip if:** You want full-sleeve sensation and contraction — the Max 2 is the better choice.
