Wand vibrators have been dominated by the Hitachi Magic Wand for decades. The We-Vibe Wand 2 doesn't dethrone it — the Magic Wand's motor is still a benchmark — but it makes a strong case for $149 over the Magic Wand Original's lower price point.

The advantage is material quality: full silicone head, IPX6 waterproofing, and app connectivity that the Magic Wand doesn't offer.

---

## Motor & Power

The Wand 2's motor delivers deep, broad vibration through the oversized silicone head. It's powerful — more than enough for the vast majority of users. It won't match the raw maximum intensity of a plug-in Magic Wand, but it approaches it closely and exceeds it in vibration quality (rumbly vs. buzzy).

**10 intensity levels + 10 pattern modes:** More settings than most users will explore. The bottom three intensities are genuinely usable for warm-up; the top three are intense by any standard.

---

## Silicone Head: The Key Upgrade

The Wand 2's head is fully covered in medical-grade silicone — body-safe, non-porous, easy to clean. The Magic Wand's head uses a fabric-covered material that can't be fully sanitized.

This makes the Wand 2 meaningfully superior for hygiene, particularly for users who share toys or who have recurring sensitivities.

**Attachment compatibility:** We-Vibe makes several silicone attachments for the Wand 2 (sold separately) that enable internal use and other stimulation patterns.

---

## App Connectivity

Optional — the Wand 2 works perfectly without the app. The We-Connect app adds partner control and custom pattern creation, following the same interface as the Chorus and Moxie+.

For solo use, the on-device controls are simple and accessible. For partner play, the app adds meaningfully.

---

## Portability

The Wand 2 is smaller than the full-size Magic Wand — closer to the Magic Wand Rechargeable in size. It fits in a travel bag reasonably well. Rechargeable via USB-C.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Users who want a powerful wand with premium materials and hygiene standards. Also good for anyone wanting a wand with optional app control.

**Consider the Magic Wand if:** Maximum raw power is your primary criterion and material quality is secondary.

---

## Verdict

The We-Vibe Wand 2 is the best wand vibrator if build quality and hygiene matter to you. The motor is excellent, the silicone head is a genuine upgrade over fabric-covered alternatives, and the app works well. Worth the price over cheaper wands.
