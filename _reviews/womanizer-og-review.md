The Womanizer OG is conceptually ambitious: it applies the same contactless pressure-wave technology that makes Womanizer's clitoral toys effective to internal G-spot stimulation. It's a genuinely new type of sensation, and for users who've been underwhelmed by traditional G-spot vibrators, it represents a meaningful alternative.

It's also the most complex Womanizer product to use — the placement precision required for internal Pleasure Air is higher than for clitoral application.

---

## How Internal Pleasure Air Works

Traditional G-spot vibrators stimulate through direct contact and vibration. The OG's insertable head generates pressure waves inside the vaginal canal, stimulating the G-spot area without direct rhythmic contact.

The sensation is distinct from G-spot vibration: more diffuse, less buzzy, and for many users, more intense. Users who've described G-spot vibrators as "I can feel it but it's not doing much" often respond very differently to the OG's pressure-wave approach.

---

## Positioning: The Learning Curve

The OG requires more precise placement than clitoral Pleasure Air toys. The pressure head must be positioned correctly against the anterior vaginal wall (G-spot area) for the technology to work as intended.

This means the OG has a learning curve — expect to spend time with positioning before the toy performs at its best. Most users report finding the right placement within 2–3 sessions.

**Tip:** Use the OG at a moderate angle with the head curved toward the belly button. Shallow insertion (2–3 inches) is usually more effective than deeper.

---

## Flexibility

The OG's flexible neck allows the head to adapt to different anatomies and positions. This flexibility is essential for internal use — rigid insertion would require more precise initial placement and allow less adjustment.

---

## Dual End: Clitoral + Internal

The OG is a dual-ended toy: Pleasure Air on the insertable end, a vibrating clitoral stimulator on the external end. The two can be used simultaneously or independently.

The clitoral stimulator is a traditional vibration motor rather than Pleasure Air — it's good, but not at the level of the Premium 2's clitoral performance.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Users who've tried multiple G-spot vibrators without satisfying results and want to experience a genuinely different sensation approach. Also good for users who enjoy both internal and clitoral stimulation and want a single toy for both.

**Not ideal for:** Users new to internal toys, or those looking for their first vibrator (the learning curve makes it a poor starting point).

---

## Verdict

The most innovative product in Womanizer's lineup. Internal Pleasure Air is a genuinely different sensation that works for many users who haven't responded to traditional G-spot vibrators. The learning curve is real but worth it.
