## What Is the b-Vibe Rimming Plug Petite?

The b-Vibe Rimming Plug Petite is the smaller version of the Rimming Plug 2 — same rotating steel bead mechanism in the neck, same dual-stimulation design (rotation + vibration), but sized for beginners and intermediate users rather than experienced ones.

Maximum insertable diameter: 3.0cm (vs 3.8cm on the full Rimming Plug 2). That 0.8cm difference is meaningful — it's the difference between an accessible intermediate toy and one that requires prior experience.

## The Rotating Bead Mechanism (Same as Rimming Plug 2)

Three stainless steel beads embedded in the silicone neck rotate against the sphincter — creating a continuous, orbiting pressure sensation that mimics external rimming. Two rotation directions (clockwise and counterclockwise) at three speeds.

At this petite size, the rotation feels gentler — not because the mechanism is weaker, but because the smaller neck diameter means less tissue displacement. For someone trying the rimming sensation for the first time, this calibrated intensity is appropriate. Experienced users who want maximum sensation should look at the full Rimming Plug 2.

## Vibration

A separate motor in the plug body provides vibration independent from rotation — five intensities, three patterns. Both mechanisms are controlled via the included wireless remote.

Running rotation and vibration simultaneously at low-medium levels is the most accessible way to experience both. The combination doesn't overwhelm at lower settings, which is correct for a petite-sized toy where the user may be newer to the sensation.

## Design Considerations

The Petite maintains the same safety features as the full size: wide flat flared base (impossible to lose internally), sealed stainless steel beads with no exposed metal, platinum-cured silicone body.

USB rechargeable via magnetic cable — 1.5 hours of use per charge, approximately 90 minutes to recharge.

Waterproof: IPX5 (splash and shower resistant, not submersible).

## Who Should Choose Petite vs Full Size?

**Choose Petite if:** You have some anal plug experience (beginner to intermediate), are specifically curious about rotating bead sensation, or want the Rimming Plug technology at a more accessible size.

**Choose Rimming Plug 2 if:** You're experienced with intermediate anal toys (3.5cm+ diameter), want maximum rotation sensation, and can handle the larger size comfortably.

The Petite is also an excellent choice for anyone upgrading from the Snug Plug or Novice Plug who wants to try something mechanically different.

## Noise

Similar to the Rimming Plug 2 — the rotating mechanism produces a low rumble audible to the wearer. Not disruptive in a private setting but more noticeable than a simple vibrator. Factor this in if noise is a concern.

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:** Rotating bead sensation at accessible intermediate sizing · Remote control included · Premium platinum silicone · Wide safety base · Both rotation and vibration independently controllable

**Cons:** Audible rotating mechanism · Smaller neck means gentler rotation than full size · IPX5 (not fully submersible) · Price high for a petite-sized toy

## Comparison to b-Vibe Novice Plug

The Novice Plug ($55) vibrates only — no rotating beads. The Rimming Plug Petite ($115) adds the rotating mechanism. If the rimming sensation is what you're seeking, the Petite justifies the price. For pure anal plug experience with vibration, the Novice Plug at half the price is excellent.

## Verdict

The b-Vibe Rimming Plug Petite successfully brings the Rimming Plug concept to users who aren't ready for the full size. The rotating bead sensation is unique and compelling even at this scale. Recommended for intermediate users who want to explore rotational stimulation without overcommitting on size.
