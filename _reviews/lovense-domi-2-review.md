Wand vibrators occupy a specific niche: they produce more power than other vibrator types, making them effective for users who need stronger stimulation, those who find smaller toys insufficient, and anyone who wants reliable results quickly.

The Lovense Domi 2 adds app control and long-distance capability to that power formula. At $89, it's priced below most premium wand alternatives and above most budget ones.

## The Wand Advantage

Wands work differently from standard vibrators. Rather than a small motor in a narrow body, wand motors are larger and housed in a wider head that distributes vibration across a broader surface area. The result is more power, broader stimulation, and — at lower intensities — a "rumbly" quality that standard vibrators struggle to match.

The tradeoff: wands are typically larger than other vibrators, making them more suited for stationary use than wearable or partnered scenarios.

## Design & Size

The Domi 2 is a compact wand by wand standards — shorter and lighter than the Magic Wand or Hitachi. At approximately 11 inches total length with a 1.5-inch head diameter, it's manageable for one-handed use and genuinely portable (it fits in a standard toiletry bag).

The flexible neck between handle and head allows positioning without awkward wrist angles — a practical feature for extended solo use.

Materials are **silicone head** with ABS plastic body. The head is soft and flexible, which is important for comfort during use.

## Motor Power

The Domi 2's motor is strong — genuinely strong by wand standards, not just "strong for an app-controlled toy." Maximum intensity is comparable to the lower end of the Magic Wand Rechargeable's range, which is meaningful given the Magic Wand's reputation as the benchmark for raw power.

At lower intensities (1–4 out of the app's range), the Domi 2 is usable for sustained stimulation without being overwhelming. The range from gentle to powerful is wider than most wands offer.

## App Control

Full Lovense Remote app integration. The Domi 2 is the most unusual Lovense use case for long-distance control — it's larger and less wearable than the Lush 3 or Ferri — but partner control during in-person use is genuinely practical. Handing your partner their phone and having them control intensity while you focus on positioning is a different experience than managing both yourself.

Custom patterns are also more useful for wand toys than for smaller vibrators, because the power means the difference between patterns is more physically pronounced.

## Battery Life

Lovense claims 3 hours. Our tests at moderate intensity averaged **2 hours 40 minutes** — slightly under claim but among the better battery performances in the wand category. Charging via USB takes about 3 hours for a full charge from empty.

## Domi 2 vs Magic Wand Rechargeable

The standard comparison for any wand:

**Raw power:** The Magic Wand Rechargeable is more powerful at maximum. For users who need maximum wand intensity, this matters.

**App control:** The Domi 2 wins decisively. The Magic Wand has no app connectivity.

**Price:** The Domi 2 ($89) is significantly less than the Magic Wand Rechargeable ($120).

**Size:** The Domi 2 is more portable.

For users who specifically need maximum intensity and don't care about app control: Magic Wand. For users who want strong stimulation with app control and better portability: Domi 2.

## Attachments

The Domi 2 is compatible with standard wand attachments (G-spot, couples, anal). These are sold separately and expand the toy's use cases significantly. Lovense sells proprietary attachments; many standard wand attachments also fit the head diameter.

## Who Is the Domi 2 Best For?

- Users who need stronger stimulation than standard vibrators provide
- Anyone who wants wand power with app control
- Couples who want partner-controlled intensity during use
- Buyers upgrading from a buzzy budget vibrator and wanting genuine power

## Our Verdict

The Lovense Domi 2 hits a sweet spot that the Magic Wand can't match: strong wand power with Lovense's excellent app. If you don't specifically need the maximum intensity of the Magic Wand and want app control, the Domi 2 is the better buy.

**Recommended for:** Users who need serious power, app control enthusiasts, anyone transitioning from weaker vibrators.
**Skip if:** You specifically need maximum wand intensity (choose Magic Wand Rechargeable), or you want a wearable toy.
