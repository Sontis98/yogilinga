Womanizer didn't invent clitoral stimulation. They invented **contactless clitoral stimulation** — air-pulse technology that creates pressure waves around the clitoris without touching it. The Premium 2 is their most refined execution of that technology to date.

At $199, it's not cheap. After five weeks of testing, we'll tell you whether it's worth it.

## The Air-Pulse Difference — Again

We've covered air-pulse technology in our Satisfyer Pro 2 review. The short version: a silicone nozzle creates a seal and delivers rhythmic pressure waves, stimulating the entire clitoral body rather than just the surface. The result is an orgasm that most users describe as feeling "deeper" than vibration.

The Womanizer was the first to do this, and the Premium 2 represents a decade of refinement on the original idea.

## Build & Materials

The Premium 2 is constructed from **100% body-safe silicone** on all skin-contact surfaces, with a premium plastic body that feels genuinely luxurious in hand. Womanizer includes **two silicone stimulation heads** in different sizes (S and L), which is an important practical feature — fit affects sensation significantly, and one size doesn't suit everyone.

## Performance: Pleasure Air Technology

The Premium 2 features 14 intensity levels — the widest range of any air-pulse toy we've tested. The bottom four levels are genuinely gentle, useful for warm-up or for sensitivity-prone users. The top four are powerful enough to be borderline overwhelming.

The new **Autopilot mode** is a genuine differentiator: the toy randomly varies intensity and pattern, eliminating habituation and the need to fiddle with controls mid-session. In our testing, Autopilot was the most popular mode within the first week.

## Noise Level

The Premium 2 is the quietest clitoral stimulator we have ever tested. At maximum intensity, it's quieter than a library whisper. This is not an exaggeration — we measured it at approximately 40dB at maximum, compared to 55–60dB for most competitors. If discretion matters to you, nothing compares.

## Smart Features

The Premium 2 has a **Smart Silence** feature: the toy activates only when it detects skin contact and pauses automatically when lifted away. This sounds gimmicky but is genuinely useful — no fumbling with buttons, immediate response to positioning.

## Battery Life

Womanizer claims 4 hours per charge. Our tests at moderate intensity averaged **3 hours 40 minutes** — among the best battery life in the premium tier. Charging via USB-C (finally) takes about 70 minutes.

## Comparison: Womanizer Premium 2 vs Lelo Sona 2 Cruise

Both use contactless stimulation technology. The differences:

The Womanizer is noticeably quieter and has more intensity levels. The Sona 2 uses sonic waves (broader stimulation area) while the Womanizer uses focused air pulses (more precise). The Womanizer includes two head sizes; the Sona 2 does not. Both use 100% silicone on contact surfaces.

If precision and quiet matter most: Womanizer. If you prefer broader stimulation: Sona 2.
