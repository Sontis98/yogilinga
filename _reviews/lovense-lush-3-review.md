Long-distance relationships have always required creativity. The Lovense Lush 3 has spent the past several years being the most practical creative solution available — a wearable G-spot vibrator that can be controlled from anywhere on earth via smartphone.

We tested it over six weeks, including in genuine long-distance use across time zones. Here is our complete assessment.

## What the Lush 3 Does Differently From Its Predecessor

The Lush 3 improved on the Lush 2 in two meaningful ways: the antenna design (now internal rather than protruding) and Bluetooth range (up from 6m to 9m locally). Everything else stayed the same, which is fine because everything else was already very good.

## Build & Materials

The Lush 3 is constructed from **body-safe ABS plastic** with a **silicone exterior** on the insertable portion. The tail that remains external is firm silicone with the integrated Bluetooth antenna.

Material safety is solid — silicone on skin-contact surfaces, no phthalates or BPA — but not the 100% platinum-cured silicone standard of Lelo's lineup. For internal use, this is worth noting, though Lovense's materials pass industry safety standards.

## Motor Performance

The Lush 3's motor is positioned at the bulb — the part that sits against the G-spot when inserted. At maximum intensity, it's one of the more powerful wearable motors we've tested. The 3 standard intensity levels can be augmented by custom patterns in the app, giving access to considerably more variation.

The motor has a slightly buzzy character at low intensities that becomes rumbly and satisfying at higher settings — an unusual and useful combination.

## The Lovense App

This is where the Lush 3 earns its reputation. The Lovense Remote app (iOS and Android) is the most feature-complete app in the category:

- **Unlimited range** via internet connection — tested reliably from across the world
- **Real-time intensity slider** for the controlling partner
- **Sound-activated mode** responds to voice or music
- **Video call integration** — the controlling partner can see the wearer's reaction while controlling the toy
- **Pattern editor** — create and save custom vibration sequences
- **Sync to music** feature

App stability across our testing was excellent — connection drops were rare and reconnection was fast.

## Wearability

The Lush 3 is designed to be worn during daily activities, not just bedroom use. In practice, wearability depends heavily on anatomy. For most vulva-owners in our testing panel, it stayed in place during light activity. Vigorous exercise caused some dislodging.

The external tail can be discreet under fitted clothing but is detectable under thin fabrics.

## Battery Life

Lovense claims 3.5–5 hours. Our tests at moderate intensity averaged **3 hours 20 minutes** — below the high end of the claim, but vastly better than most competitors. Charging via USB takes about 70 minutes.

## Who Is the Lush 3 Best For?

- Long-distance couples — this is its defining use case
- Wearable-curious buyers who want the app-controlled benchmark
- Anyone interested in partner-controlled play
- People who want to explore public or semi-public use

## Our Verdict

Three years after its release, the Lovense Lush 3 remains the most capable long-distance wearable available. Its competitors have caught up on hardware — nobody has matched the Lovense app. For couples separated by distance or time zones, it's the closest thing to a consensus recommendation in this category.

**Recommended for:** Long-distance couples, partner-controlled play, app-feature enthusiasts.
**Skip if:** You want 100% platinum silicone construction, or local use only (the app advantage disappears at close range).
