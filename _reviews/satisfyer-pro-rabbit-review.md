The Satisfyer Feel Good Rabbit is an honest product: it does what it promises at a price that doesn't require commitment to a category you might not enjoy. At $49 for a dual-stimulation rabbit vibrator with two motors and 30 combined settings, the value is hard to dispute.

The trade-off is motor quality. The rabbit's motors are buzzy rather than rumbly — effective for many users, but lacking the depth of sensation that premium alternatives deliver.

---

## Dual Stimulation Setup

The Feel Good Rabbit has a traditional design: curved internal shaft for G-spot stimulation, external arm for clitoral stimulation. Unlike the We-Vibe Nova 2's flexible arm, this one is fixed — the arm stays in position during insertion but may lose clitoral contact during thrusting.

**Practical reality:** For use in stationary positions (lying down, reduced movement), the arm maintains contact well. For more dynamic use, you may need to hold the arm in position. This is a limitation of the price point rather than a specific flaw.

---

## Motors

Two independent motors: one in the shaft, one in the clitoral arm. 10 speeds and 5 pattern modes per motor.

The motors are buzzy — high-frequency surface vibration rather than deep rumble. This is typical for Satisfyer's lower price points. For users who respond well to intense surface stimulation, this is fine. For users who prefer deep, rumbly vibration, the We-Vibe Nova 2 is a better choice.

**Noise:** Louder than premium rabbits at equivalent intensity settings.

---

## Internal Shaft

Insertable length: approximately 4 inches. Girth is moderate — appropriate for most users, including those new to internal toys. The curve toward the G-spot is gentle rather than pronounced.

**Material:** Body-safe silicone over the insertable portion, ABS plastic on the handle. Phthalate-free, confirmed by Satisfyer.

---

## Cleaning

Waterproof to IPX6 — shower use is fine. Soap and water on all surfaces, thorough rinse.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** First-time rabbit vibrator buyers who want to try the category without committing to a premium price. Also good for users who know they prefer intense, buzzy surface stimulation over deeper sensation.

**Upgrade to We-Vibe Nova 2 if:** Motor quality and the flexible clitoral arm are priorities. The Nova 2 costs $90 more but delivers meaningfully better sensation.

---

## Verdict

A capable entry-level dual-stimulation toy. The motors are adequate, the design is functional, and the price is genuinely accessible. Not the best rabbit vibrator — but possibly the best *first* rabbit vibrator for buyers who want to try the category affordably.

**Score: 7.8/10**
