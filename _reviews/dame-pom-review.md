## The Flexible Body

The Pom is made of a softer silicone formulation than most vibrators — it's squeezable and bendable throughout, not just at a hinge point. Press it against any surface and it conforms to that shape.

## Motor

Single motor, centrally located in the pom-pom-shaped body. The vibration is rumbly and distributed through the whole toy — when the body flexes, the vibration follows.

Dame's motors are consistently among the better quality in the category. At mid-intensity, the Pom delivers genuine depth of sensation; at maximum, it's strong without becoming painfully buzzy.

## Controls

One button on the base. Cycling through 3 speeds then 5 patterns then off. Simple, but requires remembering where you are in the cycle. No backtracking — you cycle forward only.

For users who want quick on/off control without pattern cycling, hold-to-off works from any setting.

## Size and Hold

The Pom is palm-sized — roughly the shape and size of a tennis ball cut in half. It's held against the body rather than gripped like a handle. The shape distributes grip pressure across the palm comfortably during extended use.
