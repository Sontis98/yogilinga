The Womanizer Classic 2 occupies the middle of Womanizer's lineup — above the Starlet 3 in intensity range and features, below the Liberty 2 and Premium 2 in portability and top-end intensity. At $99, it's the best value in the Womanizer lineup for users who want a home-use toy without travel requirements.

---

## 8 Intensity Levels

More than the Starlet 3's 6, fewer than the Liberty 2 and Premium 2. The range is meaningfully wider than entry-level: the bottom two levels are very gentle, the top two are strong. For most users, 8 levels provides sufficient control.

Womanizer's Pleasure Air technology is consistent across the lineup — same contactless pressure-wave mechanism, same sensation profile. The Classic 2 delivers the full technology at a mid-range price.

---

## Larger Form Factor

The Classic 2 is larger than the Starlet or Liberty — closer to the Premium 2 in size. This makes it comfortable to hold during extended use but less convenient for travel. For home use, the size is an advantage: the handle is easier to grip and the controls are more accessible.

---

## Noise Level

Consistent with Womanizer's standard — significantly quieter than Satisfyer at equivalent intensities. At maximum, barely audible past 8 feet. Not silent, but genuinely quiet.

---

## Two Nozzle Sizes

S and M nozzles included (vs. one size with the Starlet 3). Finding the right nozzle fit improves both comfort and effectiveness. Most users find M optimal; S for those with higher sensitivity.

IPX7 waterproof. Magnetic charging.

---

## Who It's For

**Best for:** Home users who want more intensity range than the Starlet 3 offers, without spending Liberty 2 prices. Also ideal as a first Womanizer for users who find Starlet-level intensity insufficient.

**Choose the Liberty 2 if:** Portability or travel use is a factor.  
**Choose the Premium 2 if:** Autopilot mode and 14 intensity levels matter.

---

## Verdict

The most sensible purchase in the Womanizer lineup for home users who want the full intensity range without paying Premium 2 prices. Reliable, quiet, and well-built.

**Score: 8.6/10**
