Most wearable vibrators vibrate. The Lovense Tenera vibrates and uses air-pulse suction technology — a combination that was previously exclusive to non-wearable, non-app-controlled devices like the Womanizer Premium.

The Tenera brings together three features that hadn't previously coexisted in one toy: suction stimulation, wearability during partnered sex, and full Lovense app control from any distance. The result is genuinely new rather than iterative.

## The Dual-Stimulation System

**Air-pulse suction:** The Tenera's mouth-shaped head uses rapid air pulses to create a suction effect around the clitoris without direct contact. This is the same mechanism used by Womanizer and Satisfyer air-pulse toys, but integrated into a wearable form. For users who find vibration-only stimulation insufficient, suction often unlocks faster and more reliable orgasms.

**Vibration:** Independent of the suction channel, the Tenera also vibrates. Both systems can run simultaneously. You can use suction alone, vibration alone, or both together — and you can adjust them independently through the Lovense app.

## Wearability

The Tenera is designed to sit against the vulva during use, including during penetrative sex. The flexible body conforms to body curves, and the magnetic charging port means no charging prong to worry about during wear.

In practice: the suction head stays in contact during gentle to moderate movement. Vigorous sex sometimes dislodges the positioning, which is a limitation shared with other wearable clitoral toys. The Lush 3 fits more securely during active sex; the Tenera's suction head requires more positioning care.

For solo or less-active partnered use, wearability is excellent.

## App Control

Full Lovense Remote integration — same ecosystem as Lush 3, Nora, Max 2, Calor. A partner controls both the suction and vibration intensity and patterns from anywhere. Long-distance partner control, sound-activation mode, and interactive content platform all work.

This is the Tenera's strongest differentiator from non-app-connected suction toys: you get the Womanizer-style suction experience but with Lovense's complete app ecosystem layered over it.

## Build & Materials

**Body:** Flexible silicone over an internal frame. The flexibility is important for wearability — the Tenera bends to conform to body shape. Body-safe silicone throughout the insertable and contact surfaces.

**Size:** Compact enough to fit discreetly against the body under clothing. The profile is slightly larger than the Ferri but similar in wearability philosophy.

**IPX7 waterproof** — safe for bath/shower use.

## Battery & Charging

Approximately 90 minutes of use at moderate intensity. Charging via magnetic USB connector; full charge in around 80 minutes. Battery life is slightly better than the Lush 3 at similar intensities.

## Tenera vs Competitors

**vs Womanizer Premium ($180):** The Womanizer has stronger suction intensity and is more refined as a suction-only device. The Tenera has app control, wearability, and vibration, and costs half as much. If you want the most powerful suction available, Womanizer wins. If you want the full package of features at a better price, Tenera wins.

**vs Lovense Ferri ($89):** Same price, different stimulation type. The Ferri is vibration-only with a magnetic clip. The Tenera adds suction. If suction stimulation appeals to you, the Tenera is clearly the better choice.

**vs Lovense Lush 3 ($119):** The Lush 3 is insertable and vibrates internally; the Tenera stimulates clitorally from outside. Different toys for different stimulation preferences. Users who prefer external stimulation will prefer the Tenera; those who want internal vibration will prefer the Lush 3.

## Who Is the Tenera Best For?

- Users who enjoy air-pulse/suction stimulation and want app control
- Couples using Lovense's long-distance sync ecosystem
- Anyone who finds vibration-only wearables insufficient
- Buyers looking for a wearable toy with more variety than vibration alone

## Our Verdict

The Lovense Tenera is the most feature-complete wearable clitoral toy at its price point. Suction + vibration + full app control in a wearable form is a genuinely useful combination, and the Lovense ecosystem integration is as strong here as anywhere in their lineup.

The suction intensity doesn't quite match standalone suction devices from Womanizer, but for a toy that also vibrates, also wears, and also syncs with a partner from any distance, the trade-off is entirely reasonable.

**Recommended for:** Suction-curious users, long-distance couples, Lovense ecosystem users, anyone bored with vibration-only wearables.
**Skip if:** Maximum suction intensity is the priority — the Womanizer Premium is stronger.
