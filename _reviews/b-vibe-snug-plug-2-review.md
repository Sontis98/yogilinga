## What Is the b-Vibe Snug Plug 2?

The b-Vibe Snug Plug series is defined by one design element that most plugs lack: a **weighted stainless steel base**. The Snug Plug 2 (the intermediate size in the series) uses this weighted base to create a constant, subtle fullness that shifts naturally with body movement.

The effect is difficult to describe unless experienced, but most users report that the weighted base transforms a static plug into something that feels alive — a gentle pressure that responds to movement without any vibration required.

## Materials and Safety

The Snug Plug 2 body is 100% platinum-cured silicone — one of the safest, most durable materials available. Non-porous (bacteria can't penetrate the surface), latex-free, and fully sterilizable by boiling for 3 minutes.

The base contains a weighted stainless steel core encased in silicone. The weight: approximately 80 grams, concentrated in the base. Stainless steel is also body-safe, non-reactive, and adds to the toy's premium feel.

No motors, no electronics — this is a pure body-safe material toy. Clean with soap and water or boil. Simple, reliable, zero battery anxiety.

## Size and Fit

Snug Plug 2 dimensions: 9.5cm total length, 3.5cm maximum insertable diameter. This puts it comfortably in the intermediate range — suitable for users who have tried smaller plugs and are ready for more presence.

The taper is well-designed: gradual enough for comfortable insertion, with a clear neck that the sphincter closes around naturally. The weighted base sits flush against the body — no awkward external protrusion, making it practical for extended wear including light activity.

## Sensation: The Weight Factor

Unweighted plugs feel static once inserted. The Snug Plug 2's weighted base creates constant movement feedback — lean forward, and the weight shifts forward; stand up, and it drops downward. Every body movement registers as a gentle, internal pressure shift.

This is particularly effective during partner sex or any physical activity — the plug's sensation is dynamic and responsive without requiring a battery or controls.

## Comparison: Snug Plug 2 vs Tantus Perfect Plug

The Tantus Perfect Plug is pure firm silicone — excellent quality, significantly cheaper ($30 vs $75). The key difference is the weight system. For pure wear comfort and static use, both are excellent. For dynamic sensation and the characteristic "fullness that moves," the Snug Plug 2 is in a different category.
