<p>Yogi &amp; Linga ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website <strong>yogilinga.com</strong> (the "Site"). ## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

- **Email address** — if you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us
- **Name** — if you submit a contact form
- **Message content** — if you send us a query or feedback

We do not collect payment information. Any purchases made through affiliate links are processed entirely by third-party retailers — we never see or store your payment details.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, certain information is collected automatically:

- **IP address** (anonymized where possible)
- **Browser type and version**
- **Operating system**
- **Pages visited and time spent**
- **Referring URL** (the page that linked you to us)
- **Date and time of your visit**

This information is collected via server logs and analytics tools (see Section 3).

### 1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies. You can control cookie behavior via your browser settings. See Section 4 for details.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Operate and improve the Site** — understand which content is useful, fix bugs, improve performance
- **Send newsletters** — only if you have explicitly subscribed; you can unsubscribe at any time
- **Respond to inquiries** — if you contact us
- **Comply with legal obligations** — including FTC affiliate disclosure requirements
- **Prevent fraud and abuse** — protect the integrity of the Site

We do **not** use your information to:

- Sell to or share with third-party advertisers for targeted advertising
- Build behavioral profiles for sale to data brokers
- Send unsolicited commercial emails

---

## 3. Analytics

We use **Google Analytics** (or a privacy-focused alternative such as Fathom or Plausible) to understand Site traffic and content performance. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

If using Google Analytics, data is processed under Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out via the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

We encourage the use of privacy-preserving analytics. If you prefer a version of this Site with zero tracking, you may use a browser extension such as uBlock Origin.

---

## 4. Cookies

We use the following categories of cookies:

| Category | Purpose | Can Be Disabled? |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Essential** | Age verification session storage, site functionality | No — site may not function correctly |
| **Analytics** | Anonymous traffic measurement | Yes — via browser settings or opt-out link |
| **Affiliate** | Tracking clicks to affiliate partners (set by third parties) | Yes — via browser settings |

**Age verification:** We use `sessionStorage` (not a persistent cookie) to remember that you have confirmed your age during a browser session. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

To manage cookies, adjust your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality.

Our Site contains affiliate links to third-party retailers. When you click an affiliate link:

- The retailer may set their own cookies on your browser
- Your purchase and payment information is handled entirely by the retailer, not by us
- We may receive a commission on qualifying purchases

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before making a purchase.

Current affiliate partners include networks such as **ShareASale**, **Awin**, and direct brand programs. Each has its own privacy policy.

## 6. Email Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter:

- We collect and store your email address
- We use it solely to send editorial content you have requested
- We do not sell or rent your email address to any third party
- You may unsubscribe at any time via the link in any newsletter email

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

- **Analytics data** — aggregated, retained indefinitely; individual-level data deleted per our analytics provider's default schedule
- **Email addresses** — retained until you unsubscribe, after which deleted within 30 days
- **Contact form submissions** — retained for up to 12 months, then deleted
- **Server logs** — retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### All Users
- **Opt out of analytics tracking** — via browser settings or the opt-out tools described in Section 3
- **Unsubscribe from email** — via the unsubscribe link in any email
- **Contact us** — with any privacy-related questions at [hello@yogilinga.com](mailto:hello@yogilinga.com)

### California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the "sale" of personal information — **we do not sell personal information**
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise your California rights, contact us at [hello@yogilinga.com](mailto:hello@yogilinga.com) with the subject line "CCPA Privacy Request."

### Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut Residents
Similar state privacy laws apply. Contact us to exercise your rights.

### European Users (GDPR)
If you access this Site from the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability. Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (analytics, site operation) and consent (newsletter). Contact us at [hello@yogilinga.com](mailto:hello@yogilinga.com) for any GDPR request.
