## Our Commitment to Transparency

Yogi & Linga ("we," "us," or "our") participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn a commission when you click a link to a product and make a purchase — at no additional cost to you.

This disclosure is required by the **Federal Trade Commission (FTC) under 16 CFR Part 255** and reflects our commitment to honest, transparent editorial practices.

## What This Means

When you see a link to a product on our site, it may be an affiliate link. If you click that link and make a purchase, we receive a small percentage of the sale price. The price you pay is **never higher** because of our affiliate relationship.

We participate in affiliate programs including but not limited to:

- **ShareASale** — including Lovehoney, Adam & Eve, and other wellness brands
- **Awin** — including We-Vibe, Womanizer, and LELO
- **Direct brand affiliate programs**

## Our Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships **never influence** our reviews, scores, or recommendations. We test products independently and report our findings honestly. A product that pays higher commission does not receive a higher score.

We will always tell you when we think a product is not worth buying, even if we could earn a commission from recommending it.

## How to Identify Affiliate Links

All affiliate links on this site are marked with `rel="nofollow sponsored"` in the HTML. We do not hide or obscure affiliate links. Product review posts that contain affiliate links include a disclosure notice at the top of the article.

## Questions

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how they affect our content, please contact us at [hello@yogilinga.com](mailto:hello@yogilinga.com).
